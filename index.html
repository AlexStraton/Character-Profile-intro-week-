Sailor Moon

Empowering Girl Warriors


About

The beloved anime character “Sailor Moon”–real name “Usagi Tsukino”—is an ordinary schoolgirl by day but, when needed, she can transform herself into the magical, powerful Sailor Moon who, with the assistance of her fellow (FEMALE!) Sailor Soldiers, is regularly called upon to save the solar system.


Princess of the Moon and guardian of Earth, Sailor Moon vows to right wrongs and triumph over evil. And that means you! She appeared first in the very first episode of the series.

Friends

Sailor Mercury

Ami Mizuno is Usagi Tsukino's most loyal friend. Usagi is the first person to see Ami as more than just her superior brain. Ami sees beyond Usagi's lack of academic skills as well. Though the two couldn't be more different, they balance one another out.


Sailor Jupiter

Makoto is the gentle giant of the group. Her large stature and superior strength can make her seem intimidating to people who don't know her, but Usagi takes a liking to her right away, befriending her as soon as she joins the same school.


Sailor Venus

Minako and Usagi could have been sisters. Their similar appearance allows Minako to pass as the Moon Princess as a decoy. As the true leader of the Sailor Guardians, Sailor Venus tends to be the most experienced in the field, and the one most willing to sacrifice herself for her princess. That's all a part of her duty.


Sailor Mars

Though Sailor Mars is the second Sailor Guardian that Usagi helps to awaken, the two aren't the closest of friends. In the anime, Rei and Usagi rarely see eye-to-eye on anything. In the manga, Rei is a bit more distant from the other Sailor Guardians as a result of her being a shrine maiden.


Love Life

Part of what makes Sailor Moon so iconic and enduring in the hearts of fans is its epic romance arc.


Princess Serenity and Prince Endymion have a kind of Romeo and Juliet story, and after their deaths and the downfall of the Moon Kingdom, they're reborn on Earth.


They're destined to always find each other.
